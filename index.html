Our foundation

Sunset Resolutions supports dispute resolution through weaving the uncomfortable nature of conflict into wellness, ritual, and regulation.

Cold rooms, with bright fluorescent lights, and a big conference room table, do not heal conflict even if it creates a resolution. The legal process has shifted the process and resolution away from the human need of care. Sunset Resolutions is born out of a goal to bring warmth and wellness to disputes, which this is missing from our current systems.

To be heard. · To be held. · To find a way forward.

My approach

I am not a courtroom mediator who happens to do wellness. I am a practitioner who believes in it.

  • i

    Wellness led, never clinical

    The set and setting matters. Warm rooms. Time outside. Wellness practices. Space to breathe between hard moments.

  • ii

    Structured

    Holistic does not mean unstructured. Rather, you will be guided safely, professionally, and with expertise.

  • iii

    Trauma-informed throughout

    Pace, consent, and emotional safety are built into every step from the first call to the final agreement.

  • iv

    Ceremony when it serves

    Ritual marks endings, beginnings, and repair. When invited, we close agreements with intention and not just signatures.
