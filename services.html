The Wendon Project

A homegrown wellness project for West Cork.

The Wendon Project is an initiative built for the place I live. This includes the Creative and Wellness Coalition and conducting Sound Meditations.

We are based at a wellness retreat space in West Cork.

How the Wendon Project shows up in West Cork.

Creative & Wellness Coalition

Local hands. Local healing.

A growing network of West Cork practitioners, therapists, yoga teachers, artists, creatives, somatic workers, and more, who gather under the Wendon Project to offer their skills in practical service for the community.

What this has looked like are pop-up wellness days, sliding-scale clinics, skills-share evenings and more. We also have had dedicated months where we pick a cause to donate to rooted in West Cork, and practitioners come together in service of that cause.

If you're a practitioner who wants to be part of it, please get in touch.

Sound-Based Meditations

Resonance as medicine.

I facilitate sound meditations using Tibetan bowls, gongs, cultural instruments, drumming, voice, and more. Sound work is one of the oldest tools we have for shifting the state of a room or a nervous system. I offer regular community sound baths and sessions for groups and post-circle integration.
