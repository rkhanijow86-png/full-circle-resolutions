Hello, I'm Raghav (Ricky) Khanijow.

I'm a mediator and facilitator trained in Alternative Dispute Resolution, with a long-standing parallel practice in holistic wellness. The work I do today is the intersection of those two lives.

I am based in West Cork, Ireland at a wellness retreat property.

Professional journey

Two paths that eventually joined.

The Professional path

My professional career began in formal dispute resolution, alternative dispute resolution, training programmes, and the structured back-and-forth of negotiated agreement. I worked in the policy realm of discrimination, harassment, and gender-based violence, as well as the prevention realm. My work took me towards healthy masculinity groups, facilitating agreements between parties, and more.

The policy training taught me how to hold a process. The work itself taught me how to hold a person.

The Wellness path

Alongside this, I was training and practicing in sound therapy, holistic facilitation, and medicinal spaces. What started as a personal practice became something I offered to others such as small community medicinal sessions, post-incident sound work, integration support after hard conversations, and more.

It became clear that these two practices weren't separate. The same nervous system sat in the mediation room and in alternative spaces. Why was I serving it differently in each? Thus, this way of dispute resolution was born.
